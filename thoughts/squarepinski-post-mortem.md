## What is Squarepinski?

<div class="text" markdown="1">
Squarepinski is a cozy jigsaw puzzle game - every piece is a square, but pieces can scale, rotate and flip!

You can get a feel for the game from a quick look faster than I can explain:

<img src="https://dotwo.games/public/assets/images/fungi.png" alt="A screenshot of a Squarepinski jigsaw puzzle in progress. Puzzle pieces of multiple sizes are visible.">

You complete each puzzle by figuring out what pieces go where (like any other jigsaw puzzle), complicated by not knowing if a given piece is too big, too small, or even mirrored entirely.

I originally developed the game in a 96-hour frenzy back in 2024 as part of the annual GMTK Game Jam, a _massive_ online community event where thousands of people build thousands of games over the course of only a few days.

I worked with two friends completely by chance - Paul and Anna-Marie, both of who I'd known at university - based entirely on Paul jumping on board on a whim:

<img src="/assets/images/thoughts/squarepinski/discord-convo.png" alt="A Discord conversation where I post about the 2024 GMTK Game Jam and Paul replies asking if I need a designer on my team" style="max-width: 100%;">

<p class="small">(I have obviously edited this to retract personal details)</p>

If I'm being completely honest, I don't know if I would have thought to ping Paul and Anna-Marie proactively, but I'm incredibly glad we ended up working together.
